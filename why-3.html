HOW MY HERO INSPIRED ME

This website was created out of inspiration from one of the Great influential and spiritual personnel of our time who I have sat under several of his teachings and ministerations growing up in The Redeemed Christian Church of God

Pastor E.A Adeboye as been my Role model,his success and influence in Christianity has been a major inspiration to me and also seeing how much and great a leader he is, showing example of how an effective leader should be. His style of leadership represent Humility, commitment and Obedience to the Service of God and also to Humanity at large. His simplicity, his timelineess, his integrity, Love for the common man and for the Salvation of lost soul everywhere he is present has placed a natural desire to become a Great Man of God as he is.
I have been intrigued by his audacity of faith and amazing spirituality. His selflessness and passion for the work of God has created a great and extreme niche for him globally. His impact to his family and world at large has alwsy motivated him to become the best version of myself and also commit also in the work of the Living God who is the Creator of the living God. I am proud to be a product of his blessings, through him I Found Jesus Christ and also I am committed to the work of God in my own little way and God has been faithful to me. I chose to write about because I want those around me to know how much of impact he has achieved since he made the decision to follow Jesus Christ our saviour.
He is indeed a Great man of honour and of valour.MY HERO PASTOR E.A ADEBOYE
